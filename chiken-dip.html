Healthy Buffalo Chiken Dip

Published by Laure Savonet May 14, 2023

Prep and Cooking Time about 40-50 minutes

List of Ingredients

4 Cans of chiken, 1 Bottle of buffalo sauce, 8 Ounces and Souce cream. One of Shredded Cheese. Ounces and Ranch Dressing.


Steps

1. Open cans of chicken, drain the water and dump them into a large bowl

2. If you're seasoning your chicken, add it now! I like to use pepper and garlic powder, with a little hint of salt.

3. If you aren't adding your seasoning, go ahead and use a big spoon, baking spatula, etc, to break up the chicken into smaller pieces.

4. If you are adding seasoning, just mix well! It'll still break up the chicken!

5. Add the Buffalo Sauce and the ranch, stirring well

6. Add the sour cream and the room temperature cream cheese.

7. It's okay if this doesn't all mix in well right away!

8. Pour everything into the crockpot, and sprinkle all of the cheese you're using on top.

9. Enjoy!
