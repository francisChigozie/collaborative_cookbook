Chicken Stuffed Shell

Published by Laure Savonet May 14, 2023

Prep and Cooking Time about 60-70 minutes

List of Ingredients

One package of chicken breasts One box of StoveTop stuffing One family sized can of cream of chicken soup One box of jumbo pasta shells


Steps

1. Cook the chicken breast in your preferred way

2. Let chicken cool, and then cut it into small pieces/p>

3. Cook the shells according to the package directions

4. Make StoveTop according to package directions

5. Combine the chicken and the stuffing

6. Make the cream of chicken soup according to the can's directions

7. Using a ladle or a measuring cup, add some of the soup into the mixture of stuffing and chicken. Just enough to help them combine together.

8. Put some of the soup into the crockpot

9. Stuff the shells and layer them into the crockpot, adding some of the soup between layers and on top.

10. Turn the crockpot on and let them cook up, will be ready anywhere from a half hour to an hour depending on the setting you have your crockpot on.

11. Enjoy!
