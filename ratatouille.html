Ratatouille

Published by Laure Savonet May 14, 2023

Prep and Cooking Time about 60-70 minutes

List of Ingredients

agricultural products • 2 egg plants, small • 3 cloves Garlic • 1 Onion, small • 2 red bell peppers • 1 tbsp Thyme, fresh • 2 cup tomatoes • 2 yellow squash, medium • 2 zucchini, medium canned goods • 2 tbsp tomato paste Bake and season • 1/4 tsp pepper • 1/2 tsp salt vinegar and oil • 1 olive oil Dairy products • 1 parmesan cheese


Steps

Preparation

1. Cut the eggplant into 2 cm cubes. Cut the courgette into rounds (not too thin so that the courgette won’t melt during cooking) Cut the bell pepper into cubes as well. Slice the onion and chop the garlic.

2. In a pot, add a some olive oil.

3. Add onion and garlic and dry for 2 minutes. Then add the eggplants and fry them for 5 minutes. Add the courgette and bell pepper and fry for 2 minutes. Pour in the tomatoes and add thyme, salt and pepper.

4. Mix everything well.

5. Close with a lid and let it cook on low heat for 1 hour. Remove the lid for the last 10 minutes of cooking.

6. Check the seasoning of the rataotuille and add a final drizzle of olive oil.
