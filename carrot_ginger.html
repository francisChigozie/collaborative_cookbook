Carrot-Ginger Soup

Published by Laure Savonet May 14, 2023

Prep and Cooking Time about 50-60 minutes (serves 4)

List of Ingredients

2 Tbsp Unsalted butter, or olive oil to substitute 1 Large Sweet Vidalia, or Yellow onion finely chopped 2 Cloves Fresh garlic, minced 1 lb (500g) Fresh carrots, cut into slices 1/4 inch thick 1/4 tsp Salt and freshly ground pepper 6 cups (1.5L) Chicken broth, or low sodium vegetable broth 1 and 1/2 tsp Peeled and grated fresh ginger 1/2 tsp Freshly grated orange zest 1 small bunch Minced fresh chives for garnish 1/2 Avocado sliced 1 or 2 Tbsp Low fat plain Greek yogurt.


Steps

1. In a large pot warm the butter or oil over medium heat.

2. When the butter has melted and the foam begis to subside, add the onionand saute until it softens and is just translucent, 12-10 minutes.

3. Add the garlic and saute until fragrant (about 30 seconds).

4. Add the carrots and salt to taste and stir to coat the carrots well with the butter or oil.

5. Add 2 cups (16 fl oz/500 ml) of the broth, increase the heat to medium high or high, and bring to a boil.

6. Reduce the heat to a simmer or low heat, and cover.

7. Cook until the carrots are tender when pierced with the tip of a knife, about 20 minutes.

8. Uncover, remove from the heat and let cool slightly.

9. Working in batches, puree the soup in a blender. Optional is to only puree 3/4 of the soup, and leave 1/4 whole to add a little texture.

10. Return to the pot and warm over medium-low heat.

11. Gradually whisk in the remaining 4 cups broth.

12. Stir in the ginger and orange zest.

13. Reheat gently, stirring occasionally, until the soup is hot, about 10 minutes.

14. Season with salt and pepper lightly, or let each person season their own to taste.
